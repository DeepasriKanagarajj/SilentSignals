How Silent Signals Protects You

A step-by-step guide to using our covert tools safely

1

Get Your Safety Toolkit

Download our free symbol pack that works like a normal emoji keyboard. To outsiders, it looks like cute stickers - only you and your trusted allies know the secret meanings.

2

Signal Discreetly

Use our symbols in normal conversations when you need help. They look innocent to anyone monitoring your communications.

Example: Text "Apples $2.99🍎" to signal gaslighting. Your ally sees the alert while abuser sees shopping talk.

3

Document Evidence Safely

Open our "Weather Forecast" app and tap record during abuse. The system saves the audio as an innocent conversation while preserving the real evidence.

4

Receive Community Support

When multiple distress signals cluster nearby, our Adiuvo system notifies helpers in the area who can provide discreet support.
